Main Page Arknights: Endfield Honkai Star Rail Wuthering Waves

Story & Info

Release date: 22.01.2026

The story takes place in a fictional world of Talos-II full of people with animal characteristics. While there are certain sci-fi elements such as highly advanced technology or space travel and colonization, the world itself is much more fantasy-like.

We play as the CEO of Endfield industries, Endministrator, who just woke up from a long sleep and now suffers amnesia. Together with our allies form Endfield we work on helping those in need using the power of Originium Arts created by the Endministrator. At the same time we fight against monsters and enemies who are spreading chaos accross the world.

Battle Mechanics

Arknights: Endfield features real-time action combat where players directly control one character. What makes it unique is the fact that other members of the team are on field at the same time and also fight under AI control with the ability to switch to them anytime.

For seamless fight players utilize the characters' attacks such as Battle Skills which in turn cost SP that regenerates overtime, dodge mechanics which cost stamina, Combo Skills with other team members or elemental states. A successful fight therefore needs not only skill but also putting together a team with good synergy.
