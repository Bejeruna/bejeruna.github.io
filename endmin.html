Endministrator

Info

Race: Undisclosed
Weapon: Sword
Element: Physical
Level: 60/90
Attributes: Agility (Main), Strength (Secondary)
Role: Main damage dealer, crowd control, consumes vulnerability stacks

Stats

Attack: 2448
Health: 4541
Defense: 88

Skills

Basic Attack: An attack with up to 5 sequences that deals Physical DMG. As the controlled operator, Final Strike also deals 18 Stagger. Basic attack performed near a Staggered enemy becomes a finisher attack that deals massive Physical DMG and recovers some SP.
Battle Skill: A forceful burst of Originium Crystals that attacks enemies within the area of effect. Deals Physical DMG and applies Crush.
Combo Skill: When another operator in the team deals DMG with combo skills, rushes to the enemy's side to deal Physical DMG and attach Originium Crystals that temporarily seal them. Applying Physical Status or Vulnerability to the sealed enemy shatters the Originium Crystals and deals additional Physical DMG.
Ultimate: Bombards the ground with Originium Crystals, dealing massive Physical DMG to all enemies within a fan-shaped area. If the enemy already has Originium Crystals on them, this skill shatters the Originium Crystals and deals 1 more hit of Physical DMG.
